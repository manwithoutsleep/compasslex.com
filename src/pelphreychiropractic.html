Pelphrey Chiropractic's Specialties

Car Accidents

Our office is perfectly suited to treat individuals who have been in car accidents. By utilizing a combination of therapies such as spinal traction, muscle stimulation, ultrasound, and specific chiropractic adjustments, we are able to treat the cause of the pain, not just the symptoms!

Chronic Pain

Our chiropractic services are tailored in a dynamic and proactive approach to your physical rehabilitation. Suffering from continuous pain can leave you frustrated. Our holistic solutions can improve your symptoms while treating the root cause of your pain.
